Rules

These rules kinda only exist to explicitly maintain ICCMC as a casual SMP. Most gameplay falls under common sense rules and "be nice."

Minecraft Rules

  1. Basic Minecraft Etiquette applies.
    e.g: Don't steal from or attack a player or their property
  2. Don't harm the server itself.
    e.g: Don't spawn 3.2 million Chickens in the Spawn Chunks or use any hacks/exploits to lag/hurt the server
  3. Don't make too big a mess with your builds.
    e.g: avoid spamming random blocks on the ground and leaving it, and try to light up your builds to avoid mob spawns where possible

Discord Rules

  1. No NSFW.
    Primarily images
  2. Keep to the channel topic somewhat.
    Showcase your screenshots in #screenshots, talk about server issues in #problems, etc.
